# Focused Operating Shell Baseline

Date: 2026-04-23
Task ID: P4C-REPO-DOC-03

## 1. Canon Delta Summary

The MVP product shape now includes a focused operating shell above the hero arrears-to-notice-readiness workflow.

This is a narrow shape update, not a wedge replacement and not a doctrine reset.

## 2. Wedge Baseline (Unchanged)

The wedge remains:

- Victoria-first
- residential-only
- arrears-first
- procedural-risk-prevention-led

The shell must support this wedge, not dilute or overtake it.

## 3. Shell Inclusion Fence (Accepted)

The focused operating shell is bounded to:

- operational context
- reminder and logging continuity
- evidence and document continuity
- matter launching into the hero workflow
- structured packaging for external handoff review

This fence is intentionally category-level and does not authorize feature-matrix expansion in canon-facing docs.

## 4. Reminder Inclusion Rule (Accepted)

Reminder surfaces are allowed only when all of the following are true:

- the reminder concerns a mandatory or clearly lawful landlord-side obligation or milestone
- the trigger can be derived reliably from captured facts
- the reminder does not infer legal path selection or strategy
- the reminder does not imply all-clear, compliance certification, or legal sufficiency

## 5. Records and Continuity Posture (Accepted)

Shell records such as inspection logs, issue registers, notes, rent entries, and document storage are continuity/evidence supports.

They must not be presented as:

- legal adjudication
- tribunal/court sufficiency
- compliance certification
- official acceptance

Upload or record presence alone does not equal readiness finality.

## 6. Shell Exclusion Fence (Accepted)

The focused operating shell excludes:

- accounting integrations
- full accounting-suite behavior
- tax advice or tax optimization
- maintenance marketplace behavior
- tenant messaging platform behavior
- direct official filing/submission
- generic PM-suite sprawl
- national expansion complexity

## 7. Doctrine and Trust Boundaries (Unchanged)

- preparation remains separate from official action
- handoff remains external and separate from execution
- no legal-advice behavior
- no official-system implication or parity claim
- bond-paid status is factual visibility only, not legal sufficiency
- export remains structured packaging, not filing or acceptance

## 8. Phase and Lane Posture (Unchanged)

- Phase 4B remains the primary build/control phase.
- Phase 4C remains bounded parallel product-experience construction.
- This baseline does not imply alpha readiness.
- Lane 2 and Lane 4 authoring protections remain in force.
