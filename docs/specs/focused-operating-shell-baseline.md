# Focused Operating Shell Baseline

Date: 2026-04-22
Task ID: P4C-REPO-DOC-02

## 1. Direction Summary

Landlord Buddy now has a focused operating shell above the hero arrears-to-notice-readiness workflow.

The hero wedge remains first in product priority, flow prominence, and contributor decision-making. The operating shell is a bounded support layer that improves capture quality, continuity, and preparation discipline before external handoff.

## 2. Phase Posture

- Phase 4B remains the primary build and control posture.
- Phase 4C runs in parallel for product experience construction (journey, forms, UI/UX, branding, and launch-facing surfaces).
- This baseline does not imply alpha readiness or a phase handover.

## 3. Shell Inclusion Baseline (Accepted)

The focused operating shell may include the following bounded capabilities:

- account/profile
- property records
- tenancy records
- renter details
- active matters list
- bond-paid status visibility
- manual rent tracking
- simple rent ledger/history
- current arrears status
- lease start/end capture
- renewal/expiry reminders
- mandatory compliance reminders only within accepted legal guardrails
- inspection reminders and outcome status
- manual maintenance register
- general notes/condition/observation entries
- document/evidence vault
- matter launcher
- structured export

## 4. Shell Exclusion Baseline (Accepted)

The focused operating shell excludes:

- accounting integrations
- full accounting suite behavior
- tax advice or tax optimization
- direct official filing/submission
- tenant messaging platform behavior
- maintenance marketplace behavior
- generic PM-suite sprawl

## 5. Legal, Trust, and Marketing Guardrails

The shell does not change established doctrine:

- Preparation remains separate from official action.
- Handoff remains external and separate from execution.
- No legal-advice behavior.
- No official-system implication or parity claims.
- No reminder may imply compliance certification or an all-clear outcome.
- Bond-paid status is factual visibility only, not legal sufficiency.
- Export is packaging only and must not imply filing, tribunal acceptance, or legal sufficiency.

## 6. Hero Workflow Precedence Rule

- Arrears-to-notice-readiness remains the hero workflow and lead conversion/value path.
- Shell surfaces should feed the matter launcher and readiness preparation, not compete with or replace the hero journey.
- Contributors must avoid language or IA patterns that make the shell read as a generic property-management product.
