## Locked signal-separation rule

While BR05 is parked, this distinction remains mandatory:

- trust signal:
  - whether users engage and align with trust-cue/boundary help
- comprehension signal:
  - whether users avoid confusion around readiness, authority, and channel correctness
- conversion signal:
  - whether trigger moments move bounded support intent outcomes
- guardrail/harm signal:
  - whether overclaim-risk, trust-deficit, or negative-support patterns appear

Conversion must not be treated as sufficient proof of trust.
