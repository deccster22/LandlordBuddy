# Output, Handoff, and Evidence Shells

These Phase 4A shells keep three dimensions separate on purpose:

- `forumPath` says which arrears lane the matter belongs to.
- `outputMode` says what kind of package is being produced.
- `officialHandoffState` says whether the next step sits in an external official channel.

The `src/modules/output`, `src/modules/handoff`, and `src/modules/touchpoints` shells pass warnings, slowdown flags, and referrals through as explicit control notices. They do not imply portal parity, filing, submission, or completion.

The `src/modules/evidence` and `src/modules/audit` shells validate only stable local constraints and keep privacy, retention, hold, proof-linkage, and upload progress as visible hooks. `LOCAL_VALIDATION_READY` means local checks passed. It does not mean official acceptance or evidentiary sufficiency.
