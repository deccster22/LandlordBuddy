# ADR: Focused Operating Shell Direction

Date: 2026-04-23
Task ID: P4C-REPO-DOC-03
Status: accepted

## Context

Master Canon has accepted a narrow product-shape delta: the hero arrears workflow remains first, and a focused operating shell is now part of MVP posture.

Contributors needed a single decision record to avoid:

- drifting into PM-suite framing
- weakening reminder and records trust boundaries
- implying that shell continuity equals legal sufficiency or official action
- accidental phase/lane posture drift

## Decision

Landlord Buddy adopts a focused operating shell above the hero arrears-to-notice-readiness workflow with the following mandatory controls:

- the wedge remains Victoria-first, residential-only, arrears-first, and procedural-risk-prevention-led
- the shell remains bounded to operational context, reminder/logging continuity, evidence/document continuity, and matter launching into the hero workflow
- reminder inclusion remains constrained to mandatory or clearly lawful landlord-side obligations/milestones with triggers derived from captured facts
- reminder surfaces must not infer legal path/strategy and must not imply all-clear, compliance certification, or legal sufficiency
- records continuity surfaces (inspection logs, issue registers, notes, rent entries, document storage) remain continuity/evidence supports and must not imply adjudication or tribunal sufficiency
- shell exclusions remain explicit, including no accounting suite behavior, no tax advice/optimization, no tenant messaging platform, no maintenance marketplace, no direct official filing/submission, no PM-suite sprawl, and no national expansion complexity
- prep-and-handoff doctrine remains unchanged and official action remains external
- phase posture remains 4B primary with bounded 4C parallel construction; lane protections remain unchanged

## Consequences

- Repo docs must describe a bounded shell around a still-primary arrears wedge, not a generic PM suite.
- Canon-facing docs should stay category-level and avoid implementation-matrix detail.
- Reminder, bond, records, and export wording remain implication-sensitive review surfaces.
- Lane 2 and Lane 4 remain implementation-fidelity lanes unless explicit reopen criteria are met.
- Any shell scope broadening, implication-strengthening copy, or lane posture weakening is a review event.

## Revisit Triggers

- approved change to shell inclusion/exclusion fence
- approved change to reminder inclusion doctrine or records continuity posture
- approved change to anti-overclaim/trust boundary controls
- approved phase transition beyond 4B primary / 4C parallel posture
- approved lane-protection change for Lane 2 or Lane 4
