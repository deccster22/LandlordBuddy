## Reminder Rule (Durable)

Reminder surfaces are included only when:

- the obligation/milestone is mandatory or clearly lawful on the landlord side
- trigger logic can be derived from captured facts
- no legal-path or strategy inference is implied
- no all-clear/compliance-certification/legal-sufficiency implication is introduced

## Records Continuity Rule (Durable)

Inspection logs, issue registers, notes, rent entries, and document storage are continuity/evidence supports.

They must not imply:

- legal adjudication
- tribunal/court sufficiency
- compliance certification
- official acceptance
